The Business Carnival platform has the ability to make a virtual lobby as per industry amphibious. This is the collection of booths or stalls that would like to participate in the virtual exhibition and showcase their offering. This is the place where the audience can interact with booths and understand the overall offering provided by vendors. We offer customised designs for the exhibit hall.
